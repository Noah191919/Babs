About Me.

I'm born and raised in Los Angeles, California. I competed for Fairfax High School and head coach Harvey Kitani. I led the team to a City Championship. I received First Team All-Conference honors after averaging 13 points and 10 rebounds per game. After a sinlge season for Army at West Point(Division 1), i went to the Black Knights. Shooting nearly 60 percent from the floor despite limited minutes i was on the leaderboard for blocks in the conference and had several game winning blocks.

After that, i played one season at East LA(Junior College) for head coach John Mosely averaged 9.1 points, 7.0 rebounds and 1.1 blocks per game and led the Huskies to an overall record of 24-5 including a conference title and a trip to the SoCal playoff. Shooting 55.5 percent from the floor and 38 percent from beyond the arc, i was picked up by University of Idaho(Division 1).

For graduate school i was recruited by Daniel Eberhardt to attend Holy Names University(Division 2). I was Team captain alongside Austin Carter and i led the team in several stats averaging 12 points a game, 8 rebounds per game and 1 block. Post college career, i signed with DBI sports and had several offers in Spain and Portugal. I chose to sign with the Long Beach Blue Waves in the TBL conference in order to help out my family.
